ABOUT

Uppsala has a large number of cyclists using their bike to commute and for pleasure. However, there is very little information on how the biking patterns looks like, what paths and roads are used and when. With a smartphone with positioning techniques it is possible to track bikes and to overlay their movement paths on a map of Uppsala, with time and speed information. With enough users spread over the city it should be possible to statistically collect biking patterns. This information from all users can then be used by the city to plan for bike trail maintenance (eg sanding, salting) and by bikers to find the fastest bike path and to select already maintained paths (if such real-time information is available from Uppsala kommun).
